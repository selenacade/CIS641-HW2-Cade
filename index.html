Fun Fact About Sailing: I have been a certified sailor since 2018. I joined a sailing program when I was 13 and learned how to sail on the Detroit River. I loved the program so much that I became an assistant instructor and worked to obtain my sailing license. This program allowed me teach other children about sailing and strengthen my leadership skills.

The best part about sailing, for me, is when it is a calm summer day. When the wind and waves are calm, I love to listen to music. I normally bring my speaker and snacks out on the boat with me.
